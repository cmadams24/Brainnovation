# Refer a Technology to Our Marketplace

## 💡 Overview

The Brainnovation Marketplace is a curated ecosystem of evidence-based technologies and solutions designed to support brain health professionals in delivering better outcomes for their clients and patients.

By referring a technology to our Marketplace, you're contributing to this shared ecosystem while introducing valuable tools to the broader Brainnovation community.

## 🎯 Why Refer Technologies?

Contributing to the Marketplace allows you to:

✅ **Share valuable tools** - Introduce solutions you trust and recommend  
✅ **Support innovation** - Help proven technologies reach a wider audience  
✅ **Build partnerships** - Connect with technology providers and other practitioners  
✅ **Advance the field** - Accelerate the adoption of evidence-informed solutions  
✅ **Create value for the community** - Help peers discover tools that improve their practice  
✅ **Position yourself as a curator** - Demonstrate your expertise in evaluating brain health solutions  

## 📋 What Can You Refer?

Technologies suitable for the Brainnovation Marketplace include:

- **Assessment tools** - Diagnostic, screening, or outcomes measurement platforms
- **Clinical software** - Treatment planning, documentation, or client management systems
- **Therapeutic technologies** - Neurofeedback, biofeedback, brain training, or rehabilitation tools
- **Communication platforms** - Telehealth, virtual meeting, or client engagement solutions
- **Data analytics** - Practice management, outcomes tracking, or research tools
- **Professional development** - Training platforms, continuing education, or skill-building resources
- **Business solutions** - Marketing, scheduling, billing, or operational management tools

## ✨ What Makes a Strong Referral?

When referring a technology, consider:

- **Evidence base** - Is it supported by research or clinical evidence?
- **Ease of use** - Does it integrate well into everyday practice?
- **Outcomes** - Has it demonstrated impact on client/patient results?
- **Value** - Does it offer good value for the investment?
- **Support** - Is vendor support responsive and helpful?
- **Innovation** - Does it represent advancement in the field?

## 💡 The Referral Process

When you submit a technology referral, our team will:

1. **Review the submission** - Verify the technology and its relevance
2. **Assess the evidence base** - Evaluate scientific support and efficacy
3. **Contact the provider** - Gather additional information if needed
4. **Add to Marketplace** - Feature the technology in our curated directory
5. **Credit you** - Recognize your contribution to the community
