# Schedule Your Brainnovation Launch Call

## 🎯 Overview

Scheduling your Brainnovation Launch Call is a great opportunity to get oriented, connected, and set up for success inside the Network. This is your first major milestone in the onboarding journey!

## 📞 What to Expect

This **20-minute conversation** is designed to:

- **Walk through membership details** - Understand all the benefits and features available to you
- **Answer your questions** - Get clarity on anything about the Brainnovation Network
- **Explore your tools** - Learn how to engage with the tools, people, and opportunities available
- **Identify your goals** - Discuss what you hope to achieve within the community
- **Define next steps** - Create a simple roadmap to help you get the most out of your membership from day one

## 💡 Why Schedule This Call?

Your Launch Call is the foundation for a successful Brainnovation experience. During this personalized conversation, you'll:

✅ Get a complete orientation to the Network  
✅ Understand how to access all member resources  
✅ Connect with a Brainnovation team member  
✅ Learn about community opportunities and collaboration possibilities  
✅ Set clear intentions for your membership engagement
